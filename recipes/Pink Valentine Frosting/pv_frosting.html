Pink Valentine Frosting

Frosting for Easy Valentine Sandwich Cookies (see recipe).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 drops red food coloring

Steps

  1. Blend sugar, salt and flavoring. Add just enough water to make it easy to spread. Add 2 drops red food coloring and mix well.
